**Writing tips**

- Need to communicate to the reader with the least amount of decoration
- Keep to what is important
- Don't need to write what you tried but actually what you did
- It isn't about how much work you have done, but what is actually useful / what I have done
- Don't put too much future work, as it invites questions on why you didn't do it
- 1 paragraph on future work
- Intro & Background seperate them out
- intro: what you are gonna get (i.e what the reader will get out of this) in the paper
- Never leave the reader uncertain

- Background on data, maths of the k-means algorithm
- write the intro last
